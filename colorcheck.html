To simulate what your work will look like on LotJ, you can use LotJ color codes in the blue box below and a preview will appear to the right to reflect changes.
The 256 Color List [modal] [new tab]
The older and more commonly-used tokens are:


&g - Green
&b - Dark Blue
&c - Cyan
&z - Dark Grey
&G - Light Green
&B - Blue
&C - Light Blue
&r - Red (blood)
&O - Orange (brown)
&p - Purple
&w - Gray (default color)
&R - Light Red
&Y - Yellow
&P - Pink
&W - White
